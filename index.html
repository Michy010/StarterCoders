About StarterCoder

Bridging the gap between education and technology to create problem-solvers and job creators

Our Mission

To integrate technology in education, helping students use knowledge and skills obtained from education to solve real challenges in the community and create job opportunities.

We noticed most students study to pass exams but not to acquire knowledge. Many lack foundational tech skills and don't understand the opportunities in the tech industry.
